Sustainabilty and Environmental Awareness

Rodeo 1004 commits to assisting small business owners that upcycle recyclable items and consumers who advocate for environmental causes or enjoy upcycled DIY crafts at home. As we become more aware of the environmental and economic impacts due to the lack of human intervention to stop exploiting nonrenewable resources, Rodeo 1004 is also a platform to increase awareness and click-through rates for partnered nonprofit organizations.


  • World Wildlife Fund
  • Green America
  • Cool Effect
  • Oceana
  • Rainforest Alliance
  • etc.

Each item sold donates a certain amount of proceeds to their selected nonprofit organization (determined by the small business owner).
